Origin

Innovative aluminium solutions for modern living

Redefining Modern Living

Origin has become synonymous with the modern bi-fold door — a product that has transformed how architects and homeowners think about the boundary between interior and exterior space. Founded in the UK and now serving clients across North America, Origin designs and manufactures every product in-house, maintaining complete control over quality from raw aluminium extrusion to finished powder coat.

Every Origin product comes backed by a 20-year guarantee — one of the strongest in the industry and a testament to the confidence they have in their manufacturing process. Their thermally broken aluminium profiles exceed current energy codes, and the company's commitment to continuous innovation means the product line evolves alongside the demands of contemporary architecture.

Product Types

Origin's aluminium systems open up spaces and connect homes with the landscape.

Bi-Fold Doors

Origin's flagship bi-fold door system opens entire walls to the outside. With panels as wide as 1,200mm each and configurations up to 7 panels, the possibilities for transforming living spaces are virtually unlimited.

Aluminium Windows

Slim sightlines, superior thermal performance, and a powder-coat palette of over 150 RAL colours define Origin's window range. Casement, tilt-and-turn, and fixed configurations available to suit any design intent.

Pivot & Entry Doors

Make a statement at the threshold. Origin's pivot and hinged entry doors combine bold scale with precision engineering — available in full-height glazed configurations for dramatic light-filled entrances.
