Lepage

European engineering meets North American craftsmanship

Engineering Excellence

Lepage Millwork has built its reputation on a singular principle: that beautiful design and engineering precision are not mutually exclusive. Drawing on decades of European manufacturing expertise, Lepage brings a rigorous approach to every product — from the tolerances held during fabrication to the multi-point locking systems that ensure security and air-tightness in harsh climates.

Their North American production facilities combine old-world craftsmanship with modern CNC technology, enabling complex profiles and custom configurations at scale. The result is a product line that architects and builders trust for projects where performance specifications are non-negotiable and aesthetic details demand the finest execution.

Product Types

From tilt-and-turn windows to grand entrance doors, Lepage's range covers every architectural need.

Tilt & Turn Windows

A hallmark of European design, Lepage's tilt-and-turn windows offer two distinct opening modes in one elegant unit — ideal for ventilation control and easy cleaning from the interior.

Entrance Doors

Lepage's entrance door systems combine striking visual presence with exceptional thermal and acoustic performance. Multi-point locking hardware is standard across the entire line.

Fixed & Composite Units

For large, statement openings, Lepage fabricates fixed-light and composite window-door combinations with structural glazing options that meet the most demanding architectural specifications.
