Kolbe

Crafted for those who demand the finest in design and performance

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

For over 75 years, Kolbe has been setting the standard for premium windows and doors. Founded in Wausau, Wisconsin, the company has grown from a small millwork shop into one of North America's most respected manufacturers — all while preserving the meticulous attention to detail that defined its earliest work.

Every Kolbe product is the result of an unwavering commitment to quality: sustainably sourced wood species, hand-finished hardware, and precision-engineered frames that endure the harshest climates. Whether you're specifying a floor-to-ceiling lift-and-slide door for a mountain retreat or a suite of double-hung windows for a classic craftsman home, Kolbe delivers performance and beauty in equal measure.

Product Types

Kolbe's extensive line covers every opening in your home with precision-crafted solutions.

Casement Windows

Hinged at the side and operated by a crank, Kolbe casements provide unobstructed views and exceptional ventilation. Available in wood, clad-wood, and fiberglass options with hundreds of finish combinations.

Sliding Doors

Kolbe's lift-and-slide and multi-slide door systems blend indoor and outdoor living seamlessly. Smooth, effortless operation with thermally broken frames engineered for energy performance in extreme climates.

Specialty Shapes

From round-top arches to geometric transoms, Kolbe's custom specialty shapes are built to your exact specifications. No design is too complex — each piece is handcrafted in their Wisconsin facility.
