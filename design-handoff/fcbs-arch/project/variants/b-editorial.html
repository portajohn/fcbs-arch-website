§ 01 · Philosophy Four Corners, Charleston

A window is the hinge between a building and the world it occupies.

We believe Lowcountry light, salt air, and hurricane-grade engineering deserve the same measure of craft — from the first conversation to the final reveal. Our work is equal parts specification, curation, and follow-through.

For more than two decades, architects and builders across the Carolina coast have turned to Four Corners when a window needs to do more than close. We work in catalogs, shop drawings, and site visits — but mostly in the small decisions that compound into a finished room.

Our job is to make those decisions visible, then make them easy.

§ 05 · Principles

The partner
behind the glass.

i.

Specification expertise

Decades on the Carolina coast means we know the code, the loads, and the subtle decisions that separate a beautiful window from one that survives three hurricanes.

ii.

Craftsmanship, curated

Every line we carry is held to an exacting standard. From raw timber to finished reveal, quality is never a line-item to value-engineer away.

iii.

Full-service support

Design consultation, take-offs, order management, logistics, installation coordination — a single point of accountability from drawing to delivery.

iv.

Trusted on the coast

Architects, GCs, and homeowners return project after project. Our relationships are built on transparency, follow-through, and showing up.
